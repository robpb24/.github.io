THE STORY

What went wrong?

Nobody like interceptions, especially when you're the star reciever who's supposed to catch the ball. Unfortunately, this how it all began. Number 13- Odell Beckham Jr, a wide reciever for the New York Giants, watched in misery as his pass, thrown by quarterback Eli Manning, was taken away from him. This must have hurt him deeply as he couldn't contain his emotion. The poor net, who was already recovering from the last practicing kicker, was attacked by a helmet-swinging Odell who obviously doesn't understand the struggle of inanimate objects. The net retaliated in the only way he could by falling over onto Odell Beckham's face.
